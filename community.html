Github

You can find all CynuxSecurity code (including build scripts and website source) on our github.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or bugs to report, feel free to post on the issue tracker. We also accept pull requests of all sizes and importance.

Twitter

Follow us on Twitter! We mainly tweet about new packages, new mirrors, and major project changes.

We also accept bug reports and suggestions through Twitter. Just mention us. We'll respond.

Youtube

Follow us and subscribe on our YouTube channel! We mainly upload self-made videos about installation, configuration, and major tool usage.

We also accept your videos to our YouTube channel. Just mention us. We'll respond.

IRC

Come keep us company. We have a little community here. Anything is on topic but the large majority of discussion is related to security and/or CynuxSecurity.

If you need help, this is the best place to ask.

We also have a little bot that notifies the channel of git commits and package updates/additions.

You can find the official CynuxSecurity IRC channel here: #cynuxsecurity on Freenode

If you aren't familiar with the protocol IRC, use the webchat of Freenode. Just enter any nickname and the channel #cynuxsecurity (don't forget #) for join us now.

Blog & News

We have an on-site blog. The posts are usually more detailed than our Twitter posts and focus on more big project issues.

We also release interesting blog entries about CynuxSecurity, hacking, and security written by users. If you wish to add a blog entry please open a pull request on Github.
